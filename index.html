__about-me

I am a lifelong creative adventurer living in Portland as an elementary dance/movement teacher. I went to school for Art & Design and Art History at the University of Michigan and began my professional life as an artisan blacksmith. I later earned a Masters in Education and for the last eight years I have been working in classrooms from pre-K to college. I have taught in various roles including ESL teacher in Austria, art mentor in Detroit, paraprofessional, classroom teacher and music teacher. When I'm not teaching, I can be found at the bouldering gym, practicing drums/guitar/keys, or drawing/painting/illustrating digitally. As a lover of learning, I am thrilled to now enter the world of programming.
